## 🎤 Interview Preparation

### Google Interview Process:
1. **Online Assessment** (2 coding problems, 90 mins)
2. **Phone Screen** (1-2 rounds, 45 mins each)
3. **Onsite/Virtual** (4-5 rounds)
   - 3-4 coding rounds
   - 1 behavioral ("Googleyness")

### Microsoft Interview Process:
1. **Online Assessment** (2-3 coding problems)
2. **Phone Screen** (1 round, 60 mins)
3. **Onsite/Virtual** (4-5 rounds)
   - 3-4 coding + system design
   - 1 behavioral

### What They Test:
- **Data Structures**: Arrays, Trees, Graphs, Hash Tables, Heaps
- **Algorithms**: DFS/BFS, DP, Greedy, Binary Search, Sorting
- **Complexity**: Big O analysis (time & space)
- **System Design**: Scalability, databases, caching (for experienced)
- **Behavioral**: Leadership, teamwork, problem-solving

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Resume Tips:
1. **Use numbers everywhere**: "Improved by 40%", "Handles 1000+ users"
2. **Start with impact**: "Reduced latency by 50% by implementing caching"
3. **Be specific**: Not "used database", but "PostgreSQL with B-tree indexing"
4. **Show scale**: "10,000+ API calls/day", "500+ concurrent connections"

### Application Tips:
1. **Apply early**: First week applications open
2. **Referrals help**: Ask alumni, LinkedIn connections
3. **Customize resume**: Highlight relevant projects for each company
4. **Follow up**: Email recruiter after 2 weeks if no response

### Interview Tips:
1. **Think out loud**: Explain your thought process
2. **Ask clarifying questions**: Don't assume requirements
3. **Start with brute force**: Then optimize
4. **Test your code**: Walk through examples
5. **Practice on whiteboard**: Not just on computer
