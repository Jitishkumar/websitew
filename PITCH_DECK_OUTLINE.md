## Design Tips 🎨

1. **Keep it Simple**
   - One main point per slide
   - Minimal text, maximum impact
   - Use bullet points, not paragraphs

2. **Visual Appeal**
   - Use your app screenshots
   - Consistent color scheme (match your app)
   - High-quality images
   - Icons for visual interest

3. **Fonts**
   - Use 2 fonts max (one for headings, one for body)
   - Minimum 24pt font size
   - Bold important numbers

4. **Colors**
   - Stick to 2-3 main colors
   - Use your app's brand colors
   - Ensure good contrast for readability

5. **Data Visualization**
   - Use charts for numbers
   - Graphs for growth projections
   - Tables for comparisons

## Presentation Tips 🎤

### Before the Pitch:
- ✅ Practice 10+ times
- ✅ Time yourself (aim for 10-15 minutes)
- ✅ Prepare for questions
- ✅ Have demo ready on your phone
- ✅ Bring backup (PDF on laptop)

### During the Pitch:
- 🎯 Start with a hook (the problem)
- 💪 Show confidence (you built this!)
- 📱 Demo the app (let them see it work)
- 👀 Make eye contact
- 🗣️ Speak clearly and slowly
- ⏱️ Watch the time

### Common Questions to Prepare For:
1. "How many users do you have?"
2. "What's your user acquisition cost?"
3. "Why will people switch from Instagram?"
4. "How do you plan to monetize?"
5. "What if Facebook copies your features?"
6. "How much runway does this funding give you?"
7. "What's your biggest risk?"
8. "Why should we invest in you vs. other startups?"
